### Information collected automatically

When you visit the Blizzard Rush website, contact Blizzard Rush by e-mail, or interact with Blizzard Rush online advertising, Blizzard Rush may collect certain information by automated means, using technologies such as cookies, Web server logs, and Web beacons. This information may be used to provide a better-tailored and more personalized shopping experience, and for market research, data analytics, and system administration purposes. For instance, Blizzard Rush may use this information to determine whether you've visited our website before or if it is your first visit, to store your preferences and settings, to provide sign-in authentication, and for compliance with our legal obligations, policies, and procedures. Blizzard Rush may also collect data about your activities on our websites for use in providing advertising tailored to your individual interests.

The Blizzard Rush website may use cookies and other types of local storage (such as browser-based or plugin-based local storage). Cookies are small data files that reside in your computer's memory that help us deliver the best possible online shopping experience. This is standard practice for all e-commerce websites. Cookies facilitate every aspect of your shopping experience on thinkempire.com, from browsing and choosing products to ensure your order is processed accurately. Blizzard Rush may use “session” cookies (which do not remain on your computer after you close your browser) and “persistent” cookies (which do remain on your computer until you delete them or they expire.) Your browser may provide the ability to be notified when you receive certain types of cookies and how to restrict or disable certain cookies. Please note, however, that if you disable cookies, you may not be able to use all of the features and functions of our website.

Blizzard Rush may also use third-party Web analytics services on its website, such as those provided by Google Analytics, to help us analyze how users use our website. These providers may collect various data points associated with your visits to our website, including your IP address, and they may place cookies to help with the analysis.

Our website uses encryption technology, such as Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL"), to protect your personal information during data transport. SSL encrypts ordering information such as your name, address, and credit card number. We do not store any credit card information on our web servers. Your credit or debit card information is securely transmitted to our payment processing provider and processed in the same way it would be if you would physically be in one of our stores.
